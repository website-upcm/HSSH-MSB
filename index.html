🏛️ How to Reserve a Room (Made Easy!)

Follow these steps to reserve a room at Henry Sy Sr. Hall quickly and easily:

Step 1: Prepare a Letter ✉️

Address your request to Arsenio Claro A. Cabungcal, MD through Engr. Michael Bondoc. Include date/time, room, event nature, requesting office, UP/non-UP status, number & category of participants, and signature.

Step 2: Send the Letter 📤

Email your request to hss-msb.upmanila@up.edu.ph.

Step 3: Wait for Approval ⏳

Once reviewed by the Building Administrator, proceed if approved. Otherwise, coordinate for alternatives.

Step 4: Check Fees 💵

If maintenance or **extended service charges** apply, coordinate with the Building Manager and **Ms. Lorelie Alcazar** for the SOA (Statement of Account).

Step 5: Payment 🧾

Pay the required fees at the **UPM Cashier**.

Step 6: Send Proof of Payment 📧

Email the proof of payment to the same thread and CC lmalcazar@up.edu.ph.

✅ Done!

Your reservation is confirmed once the process is complete. Congratulations!
