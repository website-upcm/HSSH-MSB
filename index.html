🏛️ Room Reservation Guide

📧 Reservation Contact

Please send your formal reservation request letter to:

Office Email: hss-msb.upmanila@up.edu.ph

Address to: Dr. Arsenio Claro A. Cabungcal – Building Administrator

Email: aacabungcal@up.edu.ph

CC: Engr. Darren Imperial – Building Manager

Email: dsimperial1@up.edu.ph

Reservation Process Overview

Step-by-Step Reservation

Step 1: Prepare a Letter ✉️

Address request to Dr. Arsenio Claro A. Cabungcal through Engr. Darren Imperial. Include Date, Time, Room, Event Nature, and Participants.

Step 2: Send the Letter 📤

Email your request to hss-msb.upmanila@up.edu.ph

Step 3: Wait for Approval ⏳

Once reviewed by the Building Administrator, you will be notified to proceed.

Step 4: Check Fees 💵

Coordinate with the Building Manager and Ms. Lorelie Alcazar for the Statement of Account (SOA).

Step 5: Payment 🧾

Pay the required fees at the UPM Cashier.

Step 6: Send Proof 📧

Email proof of payment and CC lmalcazar@up.edu.ph

✅ Reservation Confirmed

Your room reservation is now completed.

📩 Start Reservation Email

📄 Reservation Letter Template

To make the reservation process easier, you may download the official request letter template below and simply fill in your event details.

  • Date of event
  • Time (including preparation and clean-up time)
  • Requested room or venue
  • Nature of the event
  • Requesting office or organization
  • Expected number of participants

🚨 Disaster Risk Reduction Guide

Nationwide Simultaneous Earthquake Drill (NSED)

In Case of Fire: RACE Protocol

  • R - Rescue: Rescue persons in immediate danger.
  • A - Alarm: Activate fire alarm / Call UPM Emergency (8554-8434).
  • C - Confine: Close doors/windows.
  • E - Evacuate: Proceed to muster area.

Fire Extinguisher: PASS

Pull, Aim, Squeeze, Sweep.
